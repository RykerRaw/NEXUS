📋Terms of Use▶

NEXUS FORENSICS Intelligence Suite v8 — Effective 2025

Licensed exclusively for NC GS §74C-licensed Licensed Investigators and their retaining attorneys.

Authorized Use

Limited to: (a) licensed NC Investigators; (b) attorneys under valid retainer; (c) law enforcement under lawful process.

Prohibited Uses

  • Stalking, harassment, or surveillance outside lawful scope
  • Processing data of minors without lawful authority
  • Any use violating NC §14-196.3, §14-277.3A, or any criminal statute

Disclaimer

NC law analysis is for investigative reference only — not legal advice. Consult a licensed NC attorney.

🔒Privacy Policy▶

Local Processing

This tool runs entirely in your browser. No servers, no accounts. All case data stays on your device in localStorage.

API Transmission

Data is transmitted to Google’s Gemini API (generativelanguage.googleapis.com) only when you click Generate. Governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

Zero Telemetry

No analytics, no tracking, no data collection of any kind. Not even page views.

Your API Key

Stored only in your browser’s localStorage. Never transmitted to anyone except Google’s API servers.
